Hello! We are Ishani Guha, Swarnendu Moitra and Paul Marty. We are researchers in Linguistics at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, Queen Mary University of London and University College London, respectively. Currently we are running a research survey on English.

This survey is a part of a larger project going on under the supervision of Dr. Richard Breheny of the Linguistics Department under the Division of Psychology and Language Sciences at University College London.

The name of the larger project is: The cognitive basis of pragmatic inferences
This project has been approved by the UCL Research Ethics Committee as Project ID number: LING-2013-09-29.

In our survey, we need the participation of people whose mother tongue is English and that is why we have contacted you. However, taking part in this survey is not mandatory. Please read the conditions of the survey laid out below carefully and take part in this survey, only if you agree to these conditions.

***

In order to take part in the survey you must be at least 18 years old.

You will have to complete this survey on a laptop or a desktop computer. The survey cannot be run on a mobile phone or a tablet.

There are 54 questions in the survey, in which you will be shown a sentence and a picture and then will be asked if the sentence matches with the picture. You will have to answer ‘Yes’ or ‘No’. It will take 10 minutes in total to complete the survey. This data will be used only in the statistical analysis of the linguistic research.

The data collected from you will be stored removing all the identifiers, in accordance with the procedure laid out in General Data Protection Regulation and Data Protection Act 2018 (UK). In no part of the research will your answers be identifiable with your identity information. The de-identified data may be used for linguistic research in the future and in that case we will not seek permission from you again.

In order to get paid, please make sure that you complete the survey, which will redirect you to Prolific. Please note that, if you close the browser before the completion of the survey, you cannot be compensated.

There are no known risks associated with your participation in this research beyond those of everyday life. The participants will receive no direct benefits. This research will only help answer a few relevant linguistic questions of the investigators.

During the survey, if you wish to stop taking part in it at any point, you can do so by closing the browser window. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the survey, kindly contact Ishani via this email id: ishani.guha@gmail.com
